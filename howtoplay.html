How to Play

Here you will find the instructions on how to play the game.

All your Tamagotchi's states!

On the main page, check out your Tamagotchi's needs and react accordingly.

Your Tamagotchi's health!

If your Tamagotchi's health is low, make sure to give it some medicine.

Your Tamagotchi's entertainment!

Keep your Tamagotchi entertained by playing with it whenever it's feeling bored.

Your Tamagotchi's hunger!

Don't forget to give your Tamagotchi food when it's hungry.

Your Tamagotchi's cleanness!

Give your Tamagotchi a little pampering by giving it a sweet bath whenever it gets dirty.

Make sure to look after all its needs so it can live as long as possible.
